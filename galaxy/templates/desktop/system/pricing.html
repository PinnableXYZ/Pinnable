What is the DWB token?

When you contribute to our project on Juicebox, you will receive DWB tokens in return. With a conversion rate of 1 ETH = 1000 DWB tokens, these tokens grant you access to our pinning service for decentralized websites once it becomes available. Your available domain names and storage space will be determined by the number of tokens you possess.

dwb = Decentralized Web

What is the Planetable NFT Series A?

Series A of Planetable NFTs features three distinct designs, which can be unlocked according to your contribution level. These animated APNG NFTs can be used as avatars in compatible services. Future perks will include using them to access new macOS app icons and more.
